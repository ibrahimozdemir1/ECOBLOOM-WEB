Project Introduction

Many individuals face significant challenges in selecting and caring for plants suitable for their specific environmental conditions. Home gardeners often lack access to tailored information, leading to difficulties in achieving optimal growth and resulting in plant wilting or failure. Similarly, farmers struggle to choose the most appropriate crops for their fields, which can negatively impact agricultural productivity and sustainability.

These issues are exacerbated by a lack of localized, accurate information about weather patterns and environmental factors that influence plant health. The absence of comprehensive tools that provide personalized plant recommendations based on real-time weather data creates a gap in support for both amateur gardeners and professional farmers.

Additionally, there is a notable scarcity of user-friendly applications in Turkey that combine these elements into a cohesive platform. This lack of resources results in missed opportunities for individuals to enhance their gardening experiences and for farmers to improve crop yields. Therefore, there is a pressing need for an integrated solution that can assist users in selecting the right plants, provide care instructions, and support sustainable agricultural practices. ECOBLOOM aims to address these challenges by leveraging technology to create a holistic platform for plant selection and care.
